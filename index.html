Our Philosophy

We believe in providing a safe, stimulating environment where children aged 12 months-5 years are encouraged to explore, experiment, question, imagine and create, all while busy playing. Learn more about our philosophy and classrooms here.

Programs

Southwest Parent Child Collective is a vibrant cooperative preschool run by parents and staffed by experienced teachers. Our flexible programs include a unique young toddler classroom, a toddler classroom and a preschool classroom.
