Liz Parker

I’m delighted to teach at SWPCC. Toddlers are so joyous in exploring the world. Their language is blossoming, and their early friendships are charming. I believe SWPCC has succeeded for 40 years because parents and teachers nurture a community where each child is valued for who they are. Together we create rich experiences and build trusting relationships. I use play, stories, songs, and active listening to help the toddlers grow socially and emotionally throughout their time in my classroom.

My children are SWPCC graduates and have enjoyed lasting friendships from the program. I was a member for seven years and have been a teacher and substitute for nine years. My family has received tremendous benefits: a launch pad for my kids’ success, support as parents, and our dearest friendships.

I bring my heart to caring for young children with a firm grasp of what’s appropriate for their ages and stages, creativity and playfulness. Over 25 years, I’ve worked in diverse settings, including kindergarten, day care, after-school and library programs, and assisting in grades 1 through 5. My deepest knowledge and skills are teaching two- to five-year- olds. I am also a parenting coach and speaker. My background includes a degree in Psychology and 30 post-grad credit hours in child development and working with children. My continuing education includes 300 hours in child development, parenting, and educating young children.

My children Sara and Isaac are now high school students and my husband is a forensic accountant in downtown Portland. I love reading, dancing, and sharing time with friends and family.

We offer competitive tuition rates. The following rates and fees are for the 2013-2014 school year and are voted on by the co-op families each Spring for the following year. There may be financial aid if necessary.

Registration fee:

$85 non-refundable registration fee (annual)

Tuition:

$103 per month X number of days your child is at co-op without their parent. Example: if your child attends SWPCC on a three day/week schedule, you pay for two of those days because you are not billed for your shift day. 2 x $103 = $206, so your monthly tuition would be $206.

Annual Insurance coverage:

$85 (subject to change)
